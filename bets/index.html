How to play
You have 3 seconds to choose between bet, pass or life.
If you choose bet and the bomb explodes you lose 50 points, if it doesn't explodes you gain 50 points.
If you choose pass and the bomb explodes you gain 25 points, if the bomb doesn't explodes you lose 25 points.
If you choose life and the bomb explodes you lose all your points, if the bomb doesn't explodes, your points double! But if your points are negative, life will reset your points to 0.
Have fun!
