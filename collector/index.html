How to play

You upgrade your generator to produce more collectibles, and upgrade your collector to collect them faster.

The game ends when you upgrade your generator and collector to 50.

Try to do it as fast as you can.

(Items collected by you are worth twice as much as if they were picked by the collector.)

Have fun!
